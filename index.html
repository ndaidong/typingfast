7 Rules to Master 10-Finger Typing:

  1. Home Row Start: fingers on A, S, D, F and J, K, L, ;
  2. Fixed Hands, Move Fingers: no hand/wrist movement
  3. Designated Fingers: each finger has specific keys
  4. Eyes on Screen: don't look at the keyboard
  5. Return to Home Row: go back quickly after each key
  6. Slow to Fast: prioritize accuracy over speed initially
  7. Regular Practice: key to speed and accuracy
